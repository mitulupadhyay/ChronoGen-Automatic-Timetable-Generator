15 Constraints Enforced

Hard constraints cannot be broken · Soft constraints optimise quality

Hard ConstraintsH1–HU
H1Teacher can't teach two classes simultaneously
H2Class can't be in two places at once
H3Room can't host two classes at the same time
H4Class can't have two subjects in same period
H5Teacher only teaches their qualified subjects
H6Room capacity must not be exceeded
HUTeacher unavailable periods respected
Soft ConstraintsS1–S8
S1Min lectures/week per subject per class
S2Max weekly lectures per teacher
S3Limit back-to-back teaching hours
S4Same subject max once per day per class
S5Minimise gaps in class daily schedule
S6Minimise gaps in teacher daily schedule
S7Labs/gyms scheduled in correct room type
S8Morning-preference teachers get morning slots
